# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** October 4, 2025

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Climate REF application collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our web application.
We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

## Data We Collect

By default, this application does not collect any personal data from users.
The application is designed to provide access to climate model evaluation data and diagnostics without requiring user registration or personal information.

## Cookies

This application may use essential cookies to maintain state between visits and provide basic functionality.
These cookies are necessary for the application to function properly and do not collect personal information.

## Third-Party Services

The application may integrate with external services for data visualization and analysis.
Any third-party services used will be documented here, and their respective privacy policies should be reviewed.

### Error Monitoring (sentry.io)

This application uses Sentry (sentry.io) to capture and report errors that occur during operation.
Sentry helps us monitor application performance and quickly identify and fix issues.
When an error occurs, Sentry may collect technical information such as error messages,
stack traces, and system details (e.g., operating system, browser version if applicable).
No personally identifiable information is intentionally collected or sent to Sentry.

For more details on the data collected by Sentry, please refer to their [data collection documentation](https://docs.sentry.io/platforms/python/data-management/data-collected/).
